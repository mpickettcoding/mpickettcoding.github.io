About

Matthew Pickett is a student at Portland State University majoring in Computer Science. He has experience with C, C++, Java, and Python but is most familiar with C and C++. His interests include web development, artificial intelligence and machine learning, and computer networking. He specializes in data structures, object-oriented programming, computer systems, and discrete structures. He has a strong work ethic and a passion for learning new things. In his free time, Matthew enjoys travelling and exploring new places.
